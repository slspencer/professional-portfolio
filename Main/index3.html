About me

Susan L. Spencer is Founder and CEO of Seamly Systems, Inc. Sue has twenty years of experience in systems analysis and network security. She took a career break to raise a family then rejoined the tech world with her ground-breaking design technology that removes racial bias from clothing design. She has presented at graphic design conferences and conducting multi-week workshops around the world. Her leadership and management skills have guided remote teams across geographic and language boundaries.
