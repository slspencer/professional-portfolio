About Sue Spencer

Susan L. Spencer is the founder and CEO of Seamly Systems, Inc. Sue has twenty years of experience in systems analysis and network security. She took a career break to raise a family then rejoined the tech world with her ground-breaking technology that removes racial bias from clothing design. She has presented at graphics conferences, participated in future of design panels, and conducted multi-week designworkshops around the world. Her proven leadership and management skills have successfully guided remote teams separated by cultural, geographic, and language barriers.
